Featured project

Campus Connect

An all-in-one campus social platform for college students — a social feed, real-time chat, study material sharing, a notice board, push notifications, and an AI study assistant, all inside one web app that installs like a native mobile app.

Open the app Build something like this
Authentication

Signing up is verified, logging in is fast.

Email-verified signup

Students sign up with username, email, mobile, and password. A 6-digit OTP is sent to their email and expires in 5 minutes — the account is only created once it's verified. Over 30 disposable email services are blocked at signup so every account is reachable.

Simple day-to-day login

Login with username, email, or mobile plus password — no OTP needed here, so getting back into the app is quick. JWT tokens keep students signed in for 7 days.

Dashboard

A campus feed built for real student life.

Nine post types

General, Image, PDF, Notes, Question, Poll, Lost & Found, Event, and Notice — a format for whatever a student needs to share.

Full engagement

Like, comment, save, and share any post — plus delete on your own posts, and revote on polls whenever you change your mind.

Follow & notify

Follow other students and get notified the moment someone you follow shares something new.

Live stat cards

Real-time counts for Students, Notes, Notices, and Events sit at the top of the dashboard — tap Students to open the full directory.

Campus Groups

Five dedicated club pages — Mechanical Dept, Esports Club, Lit Society, Tech Fest, and Placement Cell — each with its own filtered feed.

Notifications

Instant in-app alerts via Socket.IO, backed by real browser/phone push notifications — even when the app is closed.

Study Materials & Notices

Everything the class needs, in one place.

Study Materials

Students upload notes, PDFs, and files up to 20MB, stored securely on Cloudinary and organized by department. Any student can browse and download what's shared.

Notice Board

A dedicated, department-wise space for official updates — exam schedules, workshop announcements, and other important campus news.

Discussion Room

Live chat, redesigned to feel like Discord.

Group chat

An open room for every student, with messages that auto-delete after 30 seconds to keep it focused on the live conversation.

Direct messages

Private 1-on-1 conversations between two students, with messages that auto-delete after 6 hours.

Presence & typing

A live "who's online" list with a green status dot, real-time typing indicators, and a 40-emoji picker in every chat.

Campus AI

A floating AI assistant, always one tap away.

Powered by Google's Gemini API — a "Campus AI" item in the sidebar on desktop, and a floating chat button on mobile where the sidebar becomes a bottom nav bar.

Find Notes Explain Physics GTU PYQ Attendance Events

Beyond these quick actions, students can ask Campus AI anything and chat freely.

Mobile Experience

Installs like a native app.

Installable everywhere

Add it to the home screen on Android, iPhone, or Desktop — no app store needed.

Instagram-style layout

On mobile, the sidebar becomes a bottom tab bar — Feed, Chat, Materials, Notices, Post, Profile — with touch targets sized for thumbs, no pinch-zoom required.

Push, even when closed

Notifications keep arriving even when the app is closed or running in the background.

Technology Stack

What's running under the hood.

React (Vite) React Router Tailwind CSS Node.js + Express Socket.IO MongoDB + Mongoose JWT + bcrypt Cloudinary Web Push (VAPID) Nodemailer Google Gemini API Render Vercel
